U. S. POLO ASSN.

2016-2022

As the person in charge of making new products for US Polo Assn's underwear and comfy clothes line, my job was to pick out the best designs and materials. I used to work with designers and other experts to make sure our clothes are both stylish and comfortable. By paying close attention to what customers want and keeping up with the latest trends, I make sure our clothes are top-notch. I love finding ways to make our clothes even better, setting new standards for quality and style in the fashion world.

My Role

USPA launched its line of innerwear and comfortwear in 2014. True to the parent brand, USPA innerwear provides users with premium imagery and experience in the cluttered Indian innerwear space. Currently, it has briefs, trunks, vests, tees, shorts, pants and thermals for men. It is one of the fastest growing innerwear brands and is present across all leading online and offline outlets.
